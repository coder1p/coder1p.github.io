My Design Process

A structured approach to ensure the best possible outcomes for every project.

1. Discover

User research, stakeholder interviews, and competitive analysis to understand the problem space.

2. Design

Creating wireframes, user flows, and high-fidelity mockups that are intuitive and visually compelling.

3. Prototype & Test

Building interactive prototypes and conducting usability tests to gather feedback and iterate on designs.

4. Deliver

Finalizing the design system and delivering detailed assets and specifications to the development team.
